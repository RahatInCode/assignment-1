Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"


Innovation

Bangladesh performs better in innovation outputs than innovation inputs in 2024. This year Bangladesh ranks 114th in innovation inputs. This position is the same as last year. Bangladesh ranks 92nd in innovation outputs.

Sustainability

Sustainable development in Bangladesh is the process of meeting the needs of the present without compromising the ability of future generations to do the same. This is done by working towards the United Nations' Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).

infrastructure

Bangladesh has made significant progress in infrastructure development, including roads, bridges, ports, and railways. The government is focusing on improving infrastructure to support the country's growing population and economy.

Empowerment

Empowerment in Bangladesh refers to the efforts to increase women's and girls' rights, opportunities, and representation in society. Bangladesh has made progress in these areas, but challenges remain.


The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.


Digital transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.
